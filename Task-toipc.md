## Scheduling a Meeting in Microsoft Teams

This topic describes how to schedule a meeting in Microsoft Teams from the Calendar app so that attendees receive an invite in advance.

### **Before you begin, ensure that:**

- You have a Microsoft Teams account.
- You must be signed in to the Microsoft Teams.
- You have permission to create meetings.

## Steps to Schedule a Meeting in Microsoft Teams:

1. Open **Microsoft Teams**.
2. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Calendar**.

    **Note:** The Calendar page displays your upcoming meetings and events.

3. Select **New Meeting**. (The meeting scheduling form opens)
4. Enter Meeting Details.
   - Meeting title
   - Required attendees
   - Optional attendees (if applicable)
   - Meeting date
   - Start time
   - End time

    **Note:** Ensure that all information is accurate before continuing.

5. In the description box, enter the meeting agenda.
6. Select **Save**.


>  Microsoft Teams schedules the meeting and automatically sends invitations to all participants. The meeting also appears in the calendars of attendees who use Outlook, since Teams and Outlook share the same calendar data.

To verify that the meeting has been created:

1. Open the **Calendar**.
2. Locate the scheduled meeting.
3. Confirm that the meeting details are correct.
4. Verify that invited participants have received the invitation.
